Our Story

Michael Barry is the founder of Sustain74

With 15+ years leading clean energy and sustainability at a global scale, Michael Barry has worked across RE100, SBTi, LEED, WELL, and the GHG Protocol—enabling Bloomberg LP to secure 100% renewable power, launch decarbonization programs, and embed sustainability into core strategy. Today, he uses AI-powered ESG insights to cut through noise, pinpoint priorities, and ensure your ambitions and strategy align with market expectations, customer demands, and competitor benchmarks.

My success at Bloomberg crystallized my vision for Sustain74: to bring data-driven rigor and AI-powered analysis to every company—not just a select few.

With over 15 years of hands-on ESG, renewable-energy, and decarbonization experience, I pair a passion for turning complex datasets into actionable insights with the practical know-how to leverage AI tools for measurable results.

I help companies craft ESG strategies and products that are grounded in rigorous data, sharpened through peer benchmarking, and strengthened by a clear business case built on competitive positioning and customer demand.

Along the way, I partnered with sales and product teams wrestling with fragmented clean-energy data, uncertain which technologies peers were actually deploying and which would meet customer or investor expectations. I saw proposals falter when benchmarks lacked context, and projects stall without clear evidence of what "works" in practice. That tension, and the drive to solve it at scale, led me to build Sustain74 so every organization can move from hypothesis to high-confidence decisions on clean energy, turning ESG from a compliance checkbox into a strategic accelerator for growth, innovation, and genuine impact.

After hearing countless pitches stumble over jargon and shallow metrics, I designed Sustain74 to give your sales and product teams true ESG fluency. They'll be able to:

  • Speak the ESG lexicon (from materiality to double materiality and TCFD to ESRS) so every conversation feels expert-led
  • Translate data into impact, framing benchmarks and KPIs in the terms investors, regulators, and procurement professionals value
  • Tell the story that matters, weaving sustainability outcomes into your value proposition so you move beyond features and into shared purpose

With Sustain74, your team won't just sell; they'll connect, inspiring confidence and forging partnerships built on a shared sustainability language.
