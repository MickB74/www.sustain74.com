Sample Engagements

Tech Company - Scope 3 Emissions

Objective: A mid-sized SaaS company needed to understand and report its Scope 3 emissions to meet enterprise customer demands. They had limited internal expertise.

Approach: We initiated a data collection sprint, engaging key suppliers to gather primary data. We then modeled the remaining data using industry-standard emissions factors and developed a streamlined reporting process for future cycles.

Expected Outcome: CDP-aligned report and a clear emissions reduction roadmap.

Retail Brand - Sustainable Packaging

Objective: A direct-to-consumer brand wanted to transition to sustainable packaging without compromising product safety or brand aesthetics. The goal was to reduce waste and improve customer perception.

Approach: We conducted a market analysis of alternative materials, assessing lifecycle impacts and costs. We then facilitated a pilot program with two promising options and gathered customer feedback through targeted surveys.

Expected Outcome: A phased rollout plan for new packaging with a 40% reduction in plastic.

Manufacturing Firm - Renewable Energy

Objective: A manufacturing firm aimed to procure 100% renewable electricity for its operations to meet its SBTi commitments. They needed to navigate complex energy markets.

Approach: We analyzed their energy consumption profile and modeled the financial implications of various procurement strategies (PPA, RECs, etc.). We then ran a competitive RFP process with qualified energy suppliers.

Expected Outcome: A cost-neutral transition to 100% renewable energy within two years.
